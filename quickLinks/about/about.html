About switzCodes 👋

Welcome to my corner of the web!

C

Hey there! I'm Chiemeka Evans, and welcome to switzCodes – my personal blog where I document my journey learning web development from scratch.

🚀 My Story

I started this blog because I believe learning is better when shared. As someone who's just beginning their web development journey, I understand the struggles of being a complete beginner – the confusion, the "aha!" moments, and everything in between.

Rather than keeping my learning to myself, I decided to document every step, every mistake, and every breakthrough. This way, other beginners can learn alongside me, and maybe I can help someone avoid the pitfalls I've stumbled into!

💻 What I'm Learning

Right now, I'm focused on mastering the fundamentals:

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Responsive Design Web Accessibility

My approach is simple: learn one concept thoroughly before moving to the next. No shortcuts, no skipping ahead – just solid, foundational knowledge.

🎯 My Mission

My goal is to make web development accessible and less intimidating for beginners. I write in simple terms, share my mistakes openly, and break down complex concepts into digestible pieces.

If you're also starting your web development journey, you're in the right place! Let's learn together and build amazing things on the web.

💬 What You'll Find Here

  • Beginner-friendly tutorials - No jargon, just clear explanations

🌟 Why "switzCodes"?

The name combines "switz" (a play on my approach to learning) and "Codes" (what we're all here to learn). It represents my journey from complete beginner to... well, slightly less of a beginner! 😄

🤝 Let's Connect

Learning is always better with company! If you're also on this journey, I'd love to hear from you. Share your struggles, celebrate your wins, or just say hi!

Remember: we're all beginners at something. The key is to start, stay consistent, and never stop learning.

Happy coding!
